Delta Airlines Check-In Policy

Book a flight Delta Airlines and get the exclusive services on board the flights. Delta offers several options to check-in for the flight seamlessly and comfortably. Customers can use Online Check-in, Mobile Check-in, Kiosk Check-in, Auto Check-in, and Curbside Check-in options.

Online Check-In:

Visit the official website of Delta Airlines and click on the 'Check In' option under the 'My Trips' tab. You can also check in through this link: www.Delta.com/checkin.

Mobile Check-In:

Open the Delta Airlines mobile application and check-in directly through the smartphone.

Kiosk Check-In:

Visit the Delta Airlines self-serve kiosk at the airport and check-in by MileagePlus card, passport, or driver's license. Auto Check-in:

This option enables customers to check-in online for the remaining segment of their itinerary and can also be done at airport self-service kiosks or through the mobile application.

Curbside Check-in:

This check-in service is offered by several airports across the Delta States. In this way, you need to provide the e-Ticket number or photo ID, Delta Airlines flight reservation code, and flight destination.

Please note:

  • For Delta and Delta Express-operated flights within the U.S., passengers without checked bags must check-in at least 30 minutes before departure.
  • For Delta and Delta Express-operated flights within the U.S., Passengers with checked bags must check-in at least 45 minutes before the flight departure.
  • For Delta and Delta Express-operated international flights, Passengers must check-in at least 60 minutes before flight departure, no matter whether they are checking baggage or not.
